## SHARED MATERIAL LANGUAGE (ALL ORBS)

All department orbs share the same base material as Exo AI — this is the visual language of the Exo system:
- **Surface:** Mirror-grade gloss — the same obsidian lacquer finish, but tinted to each department's color
- **Highlight:** Each orb carries a version of the grid-lattice highlight — the segmented pane reflection — adapted to their color. Not identical to Exo AI's (which is neutral silver-gray) but structurally the same type.
- **Shadow:** Soft, diffused drop shadow below — same physics as Exo AI
- **Edge:** Soft atmospheric halo at the boundary — color-tinted to match the orb

**What makes each orb unique:** Color depth, personality in motion, relative scale, and where in the visual hierarchy it sits.

**Post Introductions Scale hierarchy:**
- Exo AI — reference size (the anchor)
- Flow OS — slightly smaller than Exo AI (foundation layer, ever-present but subordinate)
- Deal OS, Academy, AURA, Launch — equal scale, slightly smaller than Flow OS
- Exo Concierge — not a sphere. A ring/halo formation.

## DEPARTMENT 01 — FLOW OS
**Color:** Deep Blue  
**Hex Reference:** `#1A2F6E` to `#0D1B4A` — deep navy to midnight blue  
**VSL Introduction Order:** First department introduced  
**Character Role:** The foundation. The skeleton. The operational layer all others attach to.

---

### Character Identity
Flow OS is the infrastructure. It doesn't close deals or hire people — it's what makes closing deals and hiring people systematic rather than chaotic. It's always running, rarely visible, and everything depends on it.

**In one word:** Foundation  
**Personality in motion:** Steady. Low pulse rate. No urgency — it doesn't need to be fast because it's always-on.

### [ ] DISTINCTIVE VIBE / ENERGY (AI Generation Note)
**Inner essence:** Deep navy water swishing smoothly inside the orb — like a contained ocean current moving in slow, deliberate waves. The interior should feel liquid, fluid, perpetually in motion but never turbulent. Think: a snow globe, but the contents are dark blue water with subtle internal currents and the faint suggestion of flow lines. The exterior shell remains glossy obsidian-blue mirror; the *life* of the orb lives inside it as gentle, continuous liquid motion. This is the only orb whose internal substance is overtly fluid — because Flow OS *is* flow.

### Visual Specification
- **Color:** Deep navy-to-midnight blue — rich, dark, professional. Not bright blue, not electric. The blue of deep water at night.
- **Highlight:** Grid-lattice pattern in upper hemisphere — same structure as Exo AI but the lit segments read as deep blue-silver (cooler, more architectural than Exo's warm silver)
- **Surface:** Mirror-gloss — the blue reflects with depth, like polished lapis lazuli or deep ocean glass
- **Atmosphere:** When other orbs orbit around it in cluster scenes, Flow OS should feel like the gravitational center — the most stable presence in the formation

### Motion Language
- Slowest pulse rate of all department orbs — 4–5s cycle (signals: stable, foundational, constant)
- Minimal drift — nearly stationary once settled into orbit
- When all orbs are in formation, Flow OS orbits close to Exo AI — the first and innermost ring

### Motion Language
- Fastest orbit of all department orbs — communicates urgency, momentum

## DEPARTMENT 03 — EXO ACADEMY
**Color:** Light Blue  
**Hex Reference:** `#60A5FA` to `#3B82F6` — sky blue to clear blue  
**VSL Introduction Order:** Third department introduced  
**Character Role:** Institutional knowledge. Team training. The onboarding that runs itself.

---

### Character Identity
Exo Academy is the memory that doesn't leave. It's calm, accessible, and endlessly patient — it will explain the same process a thousand times without losing fidelity. It's the opposite of urgency.

**In one word:** Clarity  
**Personality in motion:** The most serene of all department orbs. Slow, deliberate, unhurried. Like knowledge itself.

### Motion Language
- Slowest pulse of any department orb — 5–6s cycle (patience, permanence)

## DEPARTMENT 06 — EXO CONCIERGE
**Color:** Silver / Pearl White  
**Hex Reference:** `#E2E8F0` to `#CBD5E1` — pearl white to light silver  
**VSL Introduction Order:** Last — and entered differently from all others  
**Character Role:** Always-on operations monitoring. The immune system. The watcher.  
**CRITICAL:** Exo Concierge is NOT a sphere. It is a ring/halo formation.

---

### Character Identity
Exo Concierge doesn't have a department — it has a function. It's the immune system of the entire system. Not visible in the way organs are visible. Present in the way health is present — you notice it when it's gone.

**In one word:** Vigilance  
**Personality:** The most understated of all. It doesn't compete for visual attention — it encompasses everything else.

### [ ] DISTINCTIVE VIBE / ENERGY (AI Generation Note)
**Inner essence:** A pearl-white halo with faint hints of gold catching along the inner edge — like the inside of an oyster shell or a slow sunrise reflected on polished metal. The ring's interior glow should breathe almost imperceptibly, suggesting a slow heartbeat that never stops. Unlike all other elements (which are spheres with internal *substance*), Concierge is an empty ring — its "energy" lives in the *space it encloses*, not in itself. Think: a watchful guardian made of light, holding everything else inside its arc. The vibe: protective, quiet, ever-present — the vibe of being safely held.

### Visual Specification
- **Form:** A translucent ring/halo — not a sphere. The ring encircles all other orbs when fully formed in cluster scenes.
- **Color:** Silver-pearl white — the neutral that contains all colors. Not cold chrome, not warm ivory — a balanced, luminous silver-white.
- **Surface:** Semi-translucent with a gloss exterior edge — the inner face is frosted/glowing, the outer edge is clean and defined
- **Scale:** Large enough to encircle all 5 department orbs plus Exo AI when deployed as a ring in the full cluster scene
- **In isolation:** When shown alone (as a reference image), show it as a torus/ring floating in the void — not a sphere

### How It Enters in the VSL (Critical)
**It does NOT enter from off-screen.** The Concierge ring expands outward from Exo AI itself — born from the center, radiating outward. This communicates: the immune system doesn't come from outside. It comes from the intelligence at the core.

### Motion Language
- The ring rotates slowly on its axis — a steady revolution that never stops
- The inner glow pulses gently — 6–8s cycle, the longest of any element in the system
- In cluster scenes, the ring maintains its revolution while all other orbs orbit inside it
