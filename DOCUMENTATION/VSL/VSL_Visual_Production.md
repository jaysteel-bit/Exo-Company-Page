### THE EXO ORB — CHARACTER ANCHOR
```
A perfectly smooth, floating black obsidian orb. Mirror-grade gloss surface — 
jet black lacquer with maximum reflectivity. Defining feature: a structured 
grid-lattice highlight in the upper hemisphere — 4 to 6 curved segments, 
like cathedral window panes on a black pearl, reading dark silver-gray with 
deep black seams between them. Lower hemisphere near-pure black. No internal 
glow. No subsurface scatter. Hovers with a slow, breathing pulse 
(scale 1.0 → 1.03 → 1.0). When active or speaking, pulse intensifies 
slightly (1.0 → 1.04 → 1.0). Photorealistic render quality.
```
