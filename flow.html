Questions About Flow OS

Everything you need to know before your Architecture Session.

Flow OS is designed for companies doing $2M–$50M in revenue — typically 15–200 employees — where operations have outgrown spreadsheets and tribal knowledge but a full-time CTO or enterprise platform doesn't make sense yet.

You own it. The Sovereignty Packet includes all SOPs, agent configurations, credentials, and runbooks. When we hand you the keys at day 90, we walk away. No recurring fees to Exo, no lock-in.

Flow OS integrates with your current stack — Slack, HubSpot, Notion, Salesforce, Google Workspace, and 200+ more. We don't rip and replace. We unify. Though we can help you replace them if you want to. Simply let us know.

Minimal. The Architect phase requires 4–6 hours of your leadership team's time for discovery. After that, our Engineer and Conductor work alongside your operators — not instead of them. Most clients report less disruption than onboarding a single new hire.

It's the full transfer document: SOPs for every automated workflow, agent configuration docs, credentials, escalation playbooks, Exo Academy certifications for your team, and a 90-day operational guarantee. It's designed so you never need us again.

Flow OS is built to be modified by your team. The Conductor phase trains your operators to adjust workflows, add agents, and update the system themselves. If you need help later, we offer advisory hours — but you'll likely never need them.

If any workflow we built breaks or underperforms within 90 days of handoff, we fix it at no extra fee charge. Period. We also include a performance baseline so you can measure gains objectively.
