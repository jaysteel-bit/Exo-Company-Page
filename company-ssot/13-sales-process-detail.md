# Sales Process & Offer Structure

---

## The Primary Offer: X-Scale Program

**URL:** exoent.co/x-scale  
**Tagline:** "We install a self-running AI department in your company in 90 days."  
**Availability:** Limited to 8 partners per month

The X-Scale Program is the front-door offer for Exo Enterprise's BOM/T delivery model. It is high-touch, application-based, and scarcity-gated.

## Pricing Structure

| Phase | Description | Range |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Build | One-time implementation fee | $25K–$75K |
| Operate | Monthly co-management / advisory | $3K–$10K/month |
| Transfer Add-on | SaaS maintenance subscription | $1K–$3K/month |

**Pricing philosophy:** Custom-quoted based on scope. We don't publish a price list because we build custom departments. Prospects fill out a qualification form before seeing pricing.

**Founding Member program:** First partners get 40% off the Transfer phase.

## Key Objections & Responses

**"We tried consultants before — they gave us a PDF and disappeared."**
> That's exactly why we're different. We don't advise and leave. We implement and stay until it works. Our team is in your operations for 90 days, not delivering a deck.

**"What happens if we cancel? Do we lose everything?"**
> You own everything we build. The Sovereignty Packet — SOPs, trained team, configured systems — is yours from day one. There's no lock-in. That's the whole point.

**"How is this different from hiring another consultant who creates dependency?"**
> Dependency is the opposite of what we build. Every system we install is designed so your team can run it without us. When we leave, you run faster than before — without us in the room.

**"We tried automation before and it created more work."**
> Most automation fails because it's bolted onto broken systems. We audit the real bottleneck first, then build. Quick wins in week one. Your team feels the difference before month one is over.

**"Our team is already overwhelmed — they won't adopt another tool."**
> We run the implementation. Your team doesn't have a change management project on their plate — they have a co-pilot who shows up and makes things easier from day one.

**"What size company is this for?"**
> We're built for companies past the point where spreadsheets and tribal knowledge are failing them, but before they need a full-time CTO or enterprise platform. Typically $2M–$50M revenue.

## Funnel Flow

1. Traffic lands on index.html (homepage) or via ads
2. Announcement bar and hero CTA drive to x-scale.html
3. x-scale.html: Testimonials → What You Get → 3-Phase Explanation → Application Form → FAQ
4. Form submission → Convex + Mailchimp → Internal review → Sales call
5. Sales call → Custom proposal → BOM/T engagement begins
