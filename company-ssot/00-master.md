# Exo Enterprise — Company Master

## One-Paragraph Briefing
*(Paste this into any AI session to prime context instantly)*

Exo Enterprise is an AI-native firm that builds self-running departments for ambitious businesses. We install complete AI-powered operating systems (Flow OS), run them alongside your team until they're flawless, then hand you full ownership in 90 days — permanently removing the founder as the single point of failure. Our flagship offer is the X-Scale Program. Our product ecosystem includes Flow OS (operations oversight), Deal OS (revenue generation), Exo Academy (knowledge and training), AURA (talent acquisition), Exo Launch (marketing and creative), and Exo Concierge (executive support). We serve companies doing $2M–$50M+ ARR. Website: exoent.co. Core promise: *"Operational AI sovereignty in 90 days."*

## Sub-Brand Overview

**Steel by Exo** — Premium B2C identity ecosystem operating as a standalone subsidiary under Exo Enterprise. Product line: Steel Card (NFC physical identity card, invitation-only, 10K waitlist, Q4 2026 ship target), Steel App (mobile-first digital identity, NFC tap-to-share), Steel Profile / steel.id (verified link-in-bio replacement), Steel for Teams (enterprise/agency tier). Brand strategy follows a Veblen goods model — flagship exclusivity drives demand for the accessible line. Sites: steelaccess.co (live) · steelbyexo.com (pending). → `sub-brands/steel.md` · Strategy working notes → `working-notes/steel-strategy.md`

**Steel Global** — Multi-vertical membership universe for Steel Card and Profile holders. Built around polymathic identity — each "World" (Business, Fashion, AI, Culture, Cinema, Design, Futurism, Venture, etc.) has its own editorial voice, community, and 3-layer content system (Steel Editorial / Member Content / Curated Embeds). Membership progresses: World Member → Contributor (Steel Tag) → Featured → Editor. B2B revenue via World Sponsorships and Steel Ventures (investment arm sourcing deals from within the community). Site: steelglobal.us. Tagline: "Curating The Unimagineable." → `sub-brands/steel-global.md`

**SteelSpeaks** — Jay Steel's informal founder brand. Operates alongside the official @JaySteel CEO brand — SteelSpeaks (@SteelSpeaks) is the high-volume, raw, personality-forward channel for brand awareness and top-of-funnel reach; the official Jay Steel brand is the polished surface that converts that attention into credibility. Both run simultaneously. Content direction: business, entrepreneurship, the building process — drives traffic to Exo and Steel properties without being an ad for them. → `sub-brands/steelspeaks.md`
