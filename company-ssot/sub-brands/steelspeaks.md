# SteelSpeaks

## What SteelSpeaks Is

SteelSpeaks is the informal founder brand of Jay Steel — the personal content channel where he shares thoughts on business, entrepreneurship, and life in a raw, direct, unfiltered format. It is distinct from the official CEO brand ("Jay Steel") in tone and purpose, though both are active and used simultaneously.

**Handle:** @SteelSpeaks (new, focused founder brand)
**Contrasted with:** @Jayy.steel (existing personal accounts on X, Instagram, YouTube — currently has more followers; more raw and personal)

**Purpose:** Build personal brand awareness for Jay Steel as an individual. Drive traffic to Exo Enterprise, Steel, Steel Global, and other sub-brand properties. Establish Jay as a founder worth following — not just a company to buy from.

---

## Two-Handle Strategy

Jay operates two distinct content presences simultaneously. Understanding the difference matters for any content written under either handle.

| Handle | Format | Tone | Audience |
|--------|--------|------|----------|
| @SteelSpeaks | Structured founder content | Informal, direct, "real deal" | Aspiring founders, operators, people building |
| @Jay Steel (official CEO brand) | More formal, brand-aligned | Premium, polished, Exo/Steel-adjacent | Investors, potential clients, brand partners |

SteelSpeaks is the leverage play — higher volume, more personality, broader top-of-funnel reach. The official Jay Steel brand is the premium surface that converts that attention into credibility.

**Both will be used.** They are not competing. SteelSpeaks generates the audience; the official brand converts it.

---

## Strategic Role in the Ecosystem

SteelSpeaks sits at the top of the funnel for all Steel-family properties:

```
SteelSpeaks content
    → audience awareness
        → drives to: Exo Enterprise, Steel Card, Steel Global, Steel App
```

It is the cheapest form of brand building available — founder authenticity at scale. The goal is not to blur Jay's personal voice with the company's — it's to use his voice as a distribution channel the company doesn't have to manufacture.

---

## Content Direction (Working)

SteelSpeaks content should:
- Reflect how Jay actually thinks — not polished company copy
- Touch on business, entrepreneurship, the building process, life as a founder
- Reference Exo, Steel, and other properties where genuinely relevant — not forced
- Build the "Jay Steel" name as someone worth paying attention to independently of any company

SteelSpeaks content should not:
- Sound like an Exo ad
- Be over-produced or over-polished (defeats the purpose)
- Require explanation of who Jay is every time (build toward assumed recognition over time)
