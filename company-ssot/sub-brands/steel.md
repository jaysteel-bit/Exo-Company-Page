# Steel by Exo

## What Steel Is

Steel is the consumer-facing sub-brand of Exo Enterprise. It operates as a standalone business/subsidiary — a premium identity ecosystem built around physical and digital identity products, professional networking infrastructure, and membership experiences.

The core thesis: a Steel profile is a passport that grants entry to Steel's exclusive digital ecosystems. The Steel Card is the key. Together, they form a physical-digital identity system that bridges the two worlds.

**Parent company:** Exo Enterprise LLC
**Tagline:** "Your Identity. Your World. Verified." *(not finalized)*

## Product Line (Confirmed)

### Steel Card
NFC-enabled physical identity card. Invitation-only.
- Tap-to-share contact, social profiles, and curated links via PIN-verified reveal optional.
- First 10,000 member waitlist active
- Target ship: Q4 2026

### Steel App
Mobile-first identity platform. Digital layer before physical card rollout.
- NFC phone tap-to-share
- steel.id profile link
- Tap analytics dashboard
- Context-aware profile modes (Professional / Creative / Network / Public)

### Steel Profile (steel.id)
Verified digital identity page and link-in-bio replacement.
- Targets creators, founders, and professionals
- Aggregates social presence, commerce, and identity into one verified profile
- Competes with Linktree and Link.me — differentiated by verified membership weight, NFC bridge, and Steel Global integration

### Steel for Teams
Enterprise and agency-tier profiles for professional firms, creative studios, and talent networks. B2B adjacent.

### Steel Global *(separate entity — see `sub-brands/steel-global.md`)*
Multi-vertical membership universe for Steel Card and Profile holders. The community layer of the Steel ecosystem.

**The chain:** Steel Card → Steel Profile → Steel Global Citizenship → World Participation

## Brand Position

Steel is a premium identity brand, not a tech startup. The brand signal is exclusivity, quality, and membership weight — not features or specs.

**Brand architecture (confirmed):**
- Steel (Standard): Accessible, high-quality entry tier
- Steel+ (Premium): Enhanced features, higher-grade materials
- Steel++ (Luxury/Veblen): Extremely limited flagship items that anchor the brand's prestige
